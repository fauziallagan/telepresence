What is Telepresence Robot

The telepresence robot market is heating up. And it’s no surprise considering the changing nature of work and our increasingly global, connected lifestyles. Why limit yourself to interacting and communicating with those around you when you can use telepresence to place yourself in a remote location instantly? If you’re in the market for a telepresence robot, then you’ll want to brush up on the industry leaders and learn how they compare. With real-time navigation, 4K resolution, a fully-emotive neck, and more, it's like you're right there in the room. Unlike video conferencing, SARI Robot gives you complete control of where you need to be, whenever you need to be there autonomously as well as tele operated via cloud path planning , giving you full autonomy as if you were there in person.

Development in Health Industry

Dengan autonomous planning mode, teleoperated dan kapasitas collect data serta bisa dintegrasikan dengan device IoT untuk sensing, robot bisa difungsikan sebagai diagnostik unit di front liner faskes (fasilitas kesehatan). Menjembatani fungsi anamnesis, pemeriksaan fisik, dan biological/physiological data collecting.

Data sensor/IoT dikanalisasi di sistem analitik terintegrasi yang ada di dashboard nakes. Sehingga robot lebih berperan sebagai ekstensi pemeriksaan yang bersifat remote.
